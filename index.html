Jon Hamm

Bio
  1. Age: 47
  2. Hometown: St. Louis, Missouri
  3. In 2007, actor Jon Hamm took on his most famous character to date, playing philandering ad executive Don Draper in the American Movie Classics show 'Mad Men.'


Notable Acheivements
  • In 2011, won the Critics' Choice Television Award for Best Actor in a Drama Series
  • In 2015, Jon Won the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Drama Series for 'Mad Men'
  • In 2016, he also won the Golden Globe Award for Best Performance by an Actor in a Television Series
